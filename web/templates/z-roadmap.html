Peer-to-Peer Network

Satori Neurons are able to send information to each other in a peer-to-peer fashion.

As an initial layer of decentralization, this makes the Satori Network censorship resistent while also providing certain efficiencies inherent in direct communication.

Multisig Rewards

Daily mining rewards require multiple signatures to be approved.

This means control over mining rewards is shared, sufficiently decentralizing Satori's mining algorithm.

Additional Smart Contracts

Vote and other features should eventually be handled by automated smart contracts rather than just (albeit auditable) cryptographic signatures.

Smart contracts often have a cost which can be avoided by using cryptographic signatures instead. The results are auditable on chain, but they come with the drawback of trusting a centralized entity until results are verified. To remove the centralized component Smart Contracts must be involved.

Fully Decentralized Autonomous Organization

This includes improvements to Satori's decentralized governance system, as well as the integration of 3rd party pubsub systems, and could also include new technology.

Build the Satori Neuron

The Satori Neuron is the software running on machines around the world, comprising the Satori Network.

These neurons come equipt with a the Satori AI Engine, a wallet, the ability to allow node opperators to vote and provide information (as an oracle) to the network, and are fully automated.

Build Satori Servers and Infrastructure

Satori Servers support the Satori Network of Neurons by helping them coordinate their efforts.

Most of the Satori Servers can be thought of as scaffolding meant to be replaced by blockchain and decentralized infrastructure and protocols once their functions have been matured.

Continue to Iterate

The Neuron and the infrastructure at large need updates as the Satori AI Engine is improved and The Satori Association's decentralization efforts continue.

These improvements are incremental and also include refactoring and optimization.

Replace

Once the functions are matured they can be embodied by decentralized infrastructure such as smart contrats and the Satori blockchains.
