Satori is a network of nodes, all working together to predict the future.

Anyone can publish a data feed to the network and the nodes will predict the future of that data feed.

Each node tries to make better and better predictions of the data feeds assigned to it.

Phase 1
Public Good

Produce Public Good

Publically available predictions on all of the most popular and valuable data streams in the world. This includes government metrics, demographic statistics, price and market data, weather and envionment tracking, and more.

Scale

As the number of Satori Neurons increases the network as a whole will be able to handle more predicting data streams, and will find better correlations between them. Satori will get better and better at producing its primary goal: the Satori public good of free and open prediction of the future.

Phase 2
Market Platform

Develop Market Platform

Once matured, The Satori Network represents the skill of prediction; honed and developed by a vast number of machines around the globe. That developed machine labor is brought to market through the Satori prediction market platform. Here, buyers and sellers of future forecasting can come together. This requires building out the rest of the Blockchain protocol to support a myriad of market transactions for future forecasting.

Phase 3
Fully Decentralized

Fully Functional DAO

Remove all centralizing training wheels of Satori. Prediction of the future should be something that is as free from corruption, as defended against manipulation, and as uncensorable as it is possible to make anything. To censor predictions is to manipulate the future.
